Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

Information We Collect

MANAS collects information to provide better mental health services to our users. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email, contact details)
  • Mental health assessment data
  • Session notes and progress tracking
  • Usage data and platform interactions

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized mental health support
  • Connect you with appropriate counselors
  • Track your wellness progress
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Ensure crisis intervention when needed

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your sensitive mental health information:

  • HIPAA-compliant data handling
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • Secure data storage and backup systems
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Control data sharing preferences
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@manas-platform.com
