The Student Mental Health Crisis

Understanding the scope and impact of mental health challenges in educational environments

60% of students experience overwhelming anxiety
73% experienced mental health crisis on campus
85% feel emotionally exhausted by academic demands
41% have considered dropping out due to mental health

The Growing Challenge

Student mental health has reached a critical point, with unprecedented levels of anxiety, depression, and stress affecting academic performance and overall well-being. The transition to adulthood, academic pressures, social challenges, and uncertain futures create a perfect storm of mental health challenges.

Traditional support systems are often overwhelmed, leaving students without immediate access to care when they need it most. This gap in mental health support has led to increased dropout rates, decreased academic performance, and in severe cases, self-harm and suicide.

MANAS bridges this gap by providing immediate, accessible, and personalized mental health support through AI-powered companions available 24/7.
